FeeaSim

PUBLISHED ON OCT 22, 2024 / 1 MIN READ — PROJECTS

FeeaSim is a railway simulator.

These are FeeaSim planning to do in future:

  • Support customized gameplay that player can make route or train package by self.
  • Support model formats like .obj or .b3d.
  • More Language options.
  • Cross-system support (Linux, Mac).

Q&A:

Q: Why called “FeeaSim”?

A: “Feea” is come from “via” in English. Creator think it seems to sound like the train speeding by. And fits the content of the game specially.

“Sim”, abbreviation for Simulator.

Q: What about develop progress?

A: Pausing. Because of missing member and less time develop, we are always looking for the solution.

Recently, we are tyring to using godot game engine(C Sharp) to replace LWJGL(Java).
